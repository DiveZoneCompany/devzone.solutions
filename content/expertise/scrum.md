As a certified Professional Scrum Master, I can assist, coach or 'lead' the team in productively implementing and improving the scrum methodology. 

<!--more-->

### Scrum - My point of view

The Scrum methodology should **support** the team in planning and organizing their work well and delivering high-quality work on time.
The word 'support' is essential. Following all the scrum rules to the limit does not always benefit the team, the project, or the company. It's crucial to balance it out so it works for everyone. 

For example, in a recent project it turned out it was better to use a kanban board instead of a scrum board. This took away a lot of stress from the team, and brought focus back where it should be, getting work done!
